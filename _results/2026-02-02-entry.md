We’ve been throwing the Dance Party every winter since 2021, and y’all keep
coming back! We had 36 players this time for a robust 6 rounds of webcam Middle
School.

As always, the field was diverse – very few decks were represented more than
once or twice. Comically, however, the Top 8 was incredibly blue, with only a
single Terrageddon list not running the color. It was no surprise, then, that
Angelo’s aggressive and *highly* disruptive Mono U Fliers cut through a slate
rife with combo and took it all down. Congrats, Angelo, on your first league
win and a spot at this year’s Invitational!

[We’ve got Vintage coming up in less than 2 weeks](https://stonesmtg.com/announce/2026-04-11/),
and then the plan is to kick off
the next league in early May. It’s a good spring for Romancing the Stones!
