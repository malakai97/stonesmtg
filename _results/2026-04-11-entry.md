Our old TO Stu was in town for spring break. He’s been missing Vintage and our scene, so he put together RT41 at one of his favorite venues, Kinda Tropical. The weather was a little sketchy, and the event overlapped with the BCDL in Houston, but we had a great crew of 12 players come out.

In what was a new experience for us, RTS mainstay Ryan Bartley got in touch with Stephen Daniele, who lives just north of Austin! Stephen came and hung out at KT for the day, signing cards and shooting the shit. It was the first time he’d done a Magic signing in years, and he was the man. He even graciously marked up a foil Stroke of Genius for 1st place!

Unsurprisingly, Lurrus was looking strong. We had a dramatic mirror in the finals, but Paul’s cats, frogs, and orcs were just a *little* more powerful than Ian’s and he earned himself a spot at the Invitational – along with a hot Stroke of Genius. Congrats, Paul!
