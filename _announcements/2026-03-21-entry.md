**Romancing the Stones is turning 40!**

In an effort to give people more time to abandon their jobs and
responsibilities for a day, you’re getting almost 3 whole weeks heads up about
Vintage!  RtS 40 will be on March 21st!

![]({{site.cdn_url}}/assets/images/2026-03-21/insane_prize_support.jpg)

## Romancing the Stones 40 - Saturday, March 21st

* **Format:** Vintage
* **Location:** Austin Beerworks Sprinkle Valley (Springdale Road location)
* **Time:** 11:30 registration, 12:00 noon start
* **Entry:** $10 cash.
* **Prizes:** Prizes: 100% of entry fees paid out to top 4/8. 1st place will also win
  a spot at [2025 Invitational](/invitational). Extra prizes (pictured above)
  to the top 4: 1st place will get a playset of JPN retro foil Flusterstorm.
  2nd-4th will select from the other prizes.
* **Structure:** X rounds of Swiss (usually 4-5), followed by a cut to top 8.
* **Proxy Limit:** 100% proxy. Please make your proxies clear so opponents can easily
  identify them.
* **Registration:** There is no RVSP for this event; just show up.  Email decklists to
  romancingthestonesmtg@gmail.com
* **More Info:** Visit us in our [Discord](https://discord.gg/a9uKSEP5ya)
